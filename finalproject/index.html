The World of All Star Cheer

All star cheer is a blend of stunts, tumbling, dance, and performance. This site walks through where it started, how it grew, and how it lives in media today.

5 6 7 8

Where It Began

All star cheer grew out of traditional sideline cheer in the late 1900s when athletes and coaches wanted routines that focused on skills rather than cheering for a team on the field.

  • Gym based teams
  • Year round training
  • Skill focused routines

From Early Years to Today

As competitions grew, age groups, levels, and safety rules were created. The sport now has a detailed scoring system and international events.

  • Structured levels 1 through 7
  • Scoring for stunts, tumbling, and dance
  • Focus on safety and progressions
